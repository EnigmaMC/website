Op Skyblock

Do you want a new experience with skyblock? Something that changes the very basics? It has been done. We as staff know that people don't like repeating the same things. Nobody needs to build a cobblestone generator five times. Nor does anyone need to cut down too many trees without gain. As a result, we have built a server known as Op Skyblock or Slimefun! A revolutionary gamemode that completely changes crafting recipies and adds just so much more to the experience! Special thanks to KKosyFairiness or better known as ProKillerIV for creating this!

Factions

We also discovered that the factions community is getting bored so we decided to add something new... We completely rebuild the grounds at which you grind! On Enigma, you will find that we have special altars where you can combine the loot of killed foes to add some of the best enchants onto your necessary items! Alas, everything comes at a price. Mobs will drop reduced experience but have no fear! Each ore will no longer drop ores! They will now drop experience! Loads of it!
